Lesandro Ponciano

Lesandro Ponciano is a researcher and professor. He holds PhD. and MSc. degrees in Computer Science and a BSc. degree in Information Systems. His research and teaching interests include Human-Computer Interaction, Collective intelligence, and Distributed Computing. His work investigates human engagement and credibility in sociotechnical systems using quantitative methods, including behavioural modelling, data-driven simulation and machine learning. Some of his efforts are meta-research on crowdsourced participatory science and open science.


His email address is lesandro.ponciano@gmail.com
