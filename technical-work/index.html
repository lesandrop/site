Technical Work

List of technical work by Lesandro Ponciano .

This page does not include teaching activities. To see other activities and works see home of this website or the CV.

Paid Technical Work

  • 2025-2026: Member of the Area Advisory Committees (Comissões Assessoras de Área - CAAs) responsible for structuring, developing, and revising the ENADE at the National Institute of Educational Studies and Research (INEP), a federal government agency, Brazil. Ordinance No. 794 of 18 December 2025

  • 2023-2025: Consulting on Industrial Property Prospecting for the Provost's Office of Research and Graduate Studies at PUC Minas. This work involved producing informational materials on patents and software registration, as well as institutionalizing a prospecting process for undergraduate programs, research projects, and graduate programs at the university. Partly as a result of this work, Sociedade Mineira de Cultura, the sponsor of PUC Minas, achieved for the first time a position among the 50 largest Brazilian depositors of computer programas in 2024. The institution ranked 14th, with 39 software applications registered.

  • 2020-2025: Developed test items for the National Student Performance Exam (ENADE) at the National Institute of Educational Studies and Research (INEP), a federal government agency, Brazil.
